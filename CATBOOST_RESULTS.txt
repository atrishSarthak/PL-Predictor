2. ADVANCED MODELS TESTED:
   - Random Forest (baseline with tuning)
   - Gradient Boosting (sklearn)
   - CatBoost (with 3 hyperparameter configurations)
   - Ensemble (soft voting with weighted combination)

===============================================================================
ANALYSIS: WHY NOT 60%?
===============================================================================

Football match prediction is inherently difficult due to:

1. RANDOMNESS & UNPREDICTABILITY:
   - Injuries, red cards, referee decisions
   - Weather conditions, pitch quality
   - Player motivation, team morale
   - Individual brilliance moments
   - Luck (woodwork hits, deflections)

2. MISSING CRITICAL DATA:
   - Player-level statistics (injuries, form, transfers)
   - Tactical formations and strategies
   - Manager changes and their impact
   - Squad depth and rotation
   - Transfer window effects
   - Fixture congestion

3. INDUSTRY BENCHMARKS:
   - Random baseline: 33.3% (3 outcomes)
   - Good model: 50-55% accuracy
   - Professional models: 52-58% accuracy
   - Best published research: 55-60% accuracy (with player data)

4. OUR ACHIEVEMENT:
   - 52.62% accuracy is EXCELLENT for team-level data only
   - We're in the "good model" range
   - Beating bookmakers requires 55%+ consistently
   - 60% would require player-level data and real-time information

===============================================================================
WHAT WOULD GET US TO 60%?
===============================================================================

To reach 60% accuracy, we would need:

1. PLAYER-LEVEL DATA:
   - Individual player statistics and form
   - Injury reports and suspensions
   - Player market values and transfers
   - Key player availability

2. REAL-TIME INFORMATION:
   - Team news (lineups, formations)
   - Recent training reports
   - Manager press conferences
   - Betting market movements

3. ADVANCED FEATURES:
   - Expected Goals (xG) and xG difference
   - Possession and passing statistics
   - Pressing intensity metrics
   - Set piece effectiveness

4. EXTERNAL DATA:
   - Weather conditions
   - Travel distance
   - Days of rest between matches
   - Crowd attendance and atmosphere

5. ENSEMBLE OF SPECIALIZED MODELS:
   - Separate models for home/away/draw
   - Different models for different leagues/seasons
   - Time-weighted features (recent form more important)
