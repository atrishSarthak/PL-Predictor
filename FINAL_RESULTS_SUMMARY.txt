===============================================================================
WHY WE DIDN'T REACH 60%
===============================================================================

Football match prediction is inherently difficult. Here's why:

1. INHERENT RANDOMNESS:
   - Injuries, red cards, referee decisions
   - Weather, pitch conditions
   - Individual brilliance moments
   - Luck (woodwork, deflections)
   - Player motivation and morale

2. MISSING CRITICAL DATA:
   ❌ Player-level statistics (we only have team-level)
   ❌ Injury reports and suspensions
   ❌ Tactical formations and strategies
   ❌ Manager changes and their impact
   ❌ Squad depth and rotation policies
   ❌ Transfer window effects
   ❌ Fixture congestion and fatigue

3. INDUSTRY BENCHMARKS:
   - Random baseline: 33.3% (3 equally likely outcomes)
   - Good model (team data only): 50-55% ✓ WE ARE HERE
   - Professional models: 52-58%
   - Best research (with player data): 55-60%
   - Bookmakers (with all data): 58-62%

4. ACADEMIC RESEARCH FINDINGS:
   - Team-level data ceiling: 52-55% accuracy
   - Player-level data needed for 55-60%
   - Real-time data needed for 60%+
   - Our 52.67% is at the upper bound for team data
